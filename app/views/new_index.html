Beer Me

Beer Me uses your geolocation to pinpoint the nearest places for you to find a beer, and helps get you there ASAP.

How It Works

Log in, or create a secure account. BeerMe will use your geolocation to find the nearest venues for you to purchase a beer.

You are provided with the pertinent information on each venue. Compare venues' ranks and see their current popularity. Use BeerMe's directions feature to map your route to a chosen locale.

Save your favorite venues or store the places you want to visit in the future. They'll be there anytime you revisit the site.
